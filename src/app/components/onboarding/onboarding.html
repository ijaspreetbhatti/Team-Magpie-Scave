Welcome to Scave!

Donators list the items they want to donate and then the items are pinned to the map for scavengers to find and collect.
Anyone can remove an item from the map by marking it as collected. If an item isn’t claimed within 7 days it is automatically deleted. Don’t worry, you can always repost!
Collectors search items nearby and navigate to collect them. When a item is collected, mark as claimed to remove from the map.
Scave cannot guarantee the condition or availability of items that are posted, we are just here to help you track them down.
